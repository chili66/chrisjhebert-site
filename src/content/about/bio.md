Chris J. Hebert is a photographer and visual effects artist based in Los Angeles. His work spans feature film VFX, augmented reality experiences, and documentary photography — each discipline informing the others. He is drawn to the threshold between the real and the constructed, whether that means a long-exposure study of light on concrete or a portal tearing open the laws of physics for a Marvel blockbuster.
